About A11yForgeIT

A11yForgeIT is a demo-friendly, accessibility-first web framework template. It’s built for designers, developers, and teams who want clean, modern, and inclusive designs that meet WCAG accessibility standards.

Our Philosophy

  • Accessibility first: Every page starts with inclusivity in mind.
  • Clarity: Clean typography, balanced spacing, and easy-to-scan layouts.
  • Consistency: A simple template you can expand into any project.
