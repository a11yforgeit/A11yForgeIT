Accessibility Statement

At A11yForgeIT, we are committed to making our website accessible and usable for everyone.

We strive to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, which define how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of abilities.

While we continue improving our templates and examples, some content may not yet fully meet every accessibility requirement. We are dedicated to ongoing enhancements and welcome feedback to help us improve.

If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please contact us.
